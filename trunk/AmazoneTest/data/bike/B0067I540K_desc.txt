Veho VCC-A017-UPM MUVI HD Universal pole/bar mount for Bikes, Roll Cages, Boat Rigging with tripod mount
The universal Pole/Bar mount is the most versatile mount and will work in conjunction with the mount fittings with the MUVI HD or the tripod mount for the Waterproof case. This mount is perfect for mounting anywhere on a bike, roll cage, boat rigging or anywhere where there is a bar or pole. Ships with tripod mount and is compatible with MUVI Micro and MUVI Atom (Appropriate WPC of Handlebar mount required)
